## Key Differentiators

1. **Pay-per-contact pricing** — No competitor offers this. Eliminates risk for SMEs.
2. **AI project analysis** — Unique. No competitor analyzes student projects with AI.
3. **Personality/DISC coaching** — 25-section coaching page, far beyond any competitor.
4. **ITS institution support** — Only platform covering Italy's ITS network.
5. **Bologna Process normalization** — Critical for cross-border European hiring.
6. **Decision Pack PDF** — Unique recruiter decision-support tool.
7. **Institutional verification** — Skills verified by universities, not self-reported.


# Competitive Landscape & EU Alignment

**InTransparency — Strategic Report**
March 2026 | Confidential

---

## Executive Summary

- **No direct competitor** combines university-verified project portfolios, AI scoring, pay-per-contact pricing, and pan-EU coverage
- **JobTeaser** is the closest strategic threat with 800+ EU university integrations, but remains a job board — no verification, no portfolios
- **EU policy is moving in our direction** — the Union of Skills, Digital Credentials, and Pay Transparency Directive all reinforce our core value proposition
- **EU AI Act compliance** (August 2026) is both a regulatory requirement and a competitive moat if achieved early
- **Funding pathways exist** through ESF+ Youth Guarantee (EUR 11B) and Erasmus+ digital programs

## Platform Positioning

InTransparency replaces CVs with institution-verified project portfolios.

| | Traditional Hiring | InTransparency |
|---|---|---|
| What employers see | Self-reported CVs | Verified projects + AI scores |
| Verification | None | Institution-stamped |
| Pricing | Subscriptions or per-posting | EUR 10 per contact |
| Fraud risk | 42% of CVs contain embellishments | Zero — institution verification |
| Cost to institutions | EUR 2,500+/year (AlmaLaurea) | Free |

**Three-layer trust model:**

1. Student uploads real academic work
2. Institution verifies with one click
3. AI scores across 5 dimensions

## EU Programs & Alignment

### Programs to Integrate With

| Program | What It Provides | Our Action |
|---|---|---|
| **European Digital Credentials (EDC)** | Digitally sealed, verifiable credentials in Europass wallets | Accept and display EDC credentials |
| **ESCO Taxonomy** | 13,939 skills in 28 languages — EU's common skills language | Map our skill labels to ESCO |
| **European Student Card** | Digital student identity across 22 countries, 4M+ cards | Authenticate students via ESCI |
| **EBSI** | Blockchain-based credential verification (W3C standards) | Anchor credentials on EBSI |
| **EQF** | 8-level qualification framework across EU | Map AI scores to EQF levels |

### Regulatory Tailwinds

| Regulation | Deadline | Impact |

## Strategic Assessment

### Advantages

- Only platform with triple verification (student work + institution + AI)
- EU regulatory environment moving in our direction
- Pay-per-contact lowers barrier for employers vs. subscriptions
- Free for students and institutions — removes adoption friction
- Multi-discipline (15+), not just tech

### Threats

- JobTeaser could add verification to its 800+ university network
- Europass/EDC could evolve from infrastructure into a marketplace
- AlmaLaurea could expand its verified model beyond Italy
- Handshake has $300M ARR and is expanding internationally
- AI Act non-compliance would block our core AI scoring feature

### Opportunities

- Portfolium retiring June 2026 — capture displaced users
- EU funding through Youth Guarantee and ESF+
- ESCO integration makes us interoperable with all EU systems
- Pay Transparency Directive creates new demand for objective evaluation
- No EU-wide verified project marketplace exists yet

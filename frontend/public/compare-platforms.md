The three platforms universities and employers most often compare for entry-level hiring in Europe. Fact-by-fact. We mark where we're not a fit too — nothing hidden.

## TL;DR

- **JobTeaser** is a polished EU job board with strong company branding.
- **Handshake** is the US network-effect leader with ATS integrations.
- **InTransparency** is the verified skill graph + AI Act-native matching platform — built for EU labor law, designed around evidence instead of self-declaration.

Choose InTransparency if you want skills you can trust and a recruiting flow that's audit-trail compliant under the AI Act. Choose JobTeaser for a polished job board. Choose Handshake if you're in the US.

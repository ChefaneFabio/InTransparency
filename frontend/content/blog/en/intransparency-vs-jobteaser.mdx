## Two different bets on what career services need

JobTeaser has been the dominant career-service SaaS in Europe for two decades. They serve more than 750 schools across France, Germany, Spain, the Netherlands, and a dozen other markets. They built their position on **employer branding** — making companies look attractive to students through rich profile pages, culture videos, FAQ sections, and event integration.

InTransparency takes a different bet. We assume that what differentiates one student from another is not which companies they have heard of, but **which skills they can prove they have**. We invest in verification, evidence extraction, and the stage-to-hire workflow that follows the application.

Different bets on the same market. Here's the honest comparison.

## What JobTeaser does well

We're not going to pretend JobTeaser doesn't do important things. They do:

- **Employer brand pages with depth.** Their company profile pages are some of the most polished in the EU career-service space. Culture sections, "Inside the company" videos, founder stories. If you're a large employer trying to make a case to students, the JobTeaser canvas is genuinely useful.
- **Event integration.** Career fairs, info sessions, recruiter events tied to company profiles. Mature workflow.
- **Multi-language reach.** FR, EN, DE, ES, NL, DA. They serve cross-border European recruiting better than most.
- **Twenty years of incumbency.** Deep relationships with school career-service teams across Europe.

If your career-service strategy is built around employer branding and event programming, JobTeaser is a credible default.

## What JobTeaser doesn't do

These are gaps you can verify by reading their public product pages:

- **No skill verification from artifacts.** Student profiles are essentially CVs with a few additional fields. There is no extraction of skills from project work.
- **No stage/tirocinio CRM.** Job offers get listed and students apply, but the lifecycle that follows — convention generation, hour tracking, mid-stage and final evaluations, post-stage skill gap analysis — is not in the product.
- **No professor endorsement layer.** Faculty have no role in the JobTeaser workflow.
- **No grade normalization across European systems.** A student with a French 15/20 looks the same to a German recruiter as a German 1.7 — no automatic translation.
- **Italian compliance specifics absent.** ANVUR reporting, INDIRE alignment, PCTO tracking, MIUR-format outputs — none of these are native.

These aren't accidents. They're consequences of the product's center of gravity being employer branding rather than verification and lifecycle workflow.

## Where InTransparency fits

We lead with the things JobTeaser deprioritizes:

1. **AI-extracted skills from real artifacts.** Code, theses, project files, internship reports. The skill graph is a function of what the student built.
2. **Full stage lifecycle.** Convention Builder, mid/final evaluation framework, hour tracking, post-stage skill gap analysis, placement analytics.
3. **Three institutional tiers covered.** Universities, ITS Academy, technical and professional secondary schools — same platform, same pricing.
4. **Italian compliance native.** PCTO tracking, MIUR-format reporting, ANVUR alignment built in.
5. **AI Act-native.** Algorithm registry, right-to-explanation endpoints, audit logging — designed for Reg. EU 2024/1689 from the start.

## What Are ITS Academies?

ITS Academies (*Istituti Tecnologici Superiori*) are Italy's answer to the growing demand for highly specialized technical professionals. These post-secondary institutions offer 2-3 year programs that bridge the gap between high school and the workforce.

Unlike traditional universities, ITS programs are:
- **Industry-driven**: Curricula designed with direct input from companies
- **Hands-on**: 50-60% of learning happens through practical projects and internships
- **Employment-focused**: Over 80% of graduates find employment within 12 months

## The Six Technology Areas

ITS programs cover six strategic technology areas aligned with Italy's economic priorities:

1. **Energy Efficiency** — Renewable energy, green building, sustainability
2. **Sustainable Mobility** — Automotive, logistics, smart transport
3. **New Life Technologies** — Biotech, pharmaceutical, medical devices
4. **New Technologies for Made in Italy** — Fashion-tech, food-tech, mechatronics, furniture design
5. **Information & Communication Technologies** — Software, cybersecurity, data science, AI
6. **Innovative Technologies for Cultural Heritage** — Digital preservation, tourism-tech

## How ITS Compares to University

Many students wonder whether an ITS diploma or a university degree is the better choice. The answer depends on your goals:

**Choose ITS if you:**
- Want to enter the workforce quickly
- Prefer hands-on, practical learning
- Have a clear technical career path in mind
- Value industry connections and guaranteed internships

**Choose university if you:**
- Want to pursue academic research
- Need a degree for regulated professions (medicine, law, architecture)
- Prefer theoretical depth over practical breadth

## The Problem with Traditional Hiring

Every year, millions of graduates enter the job market armed with polished CVs and rehearsed interview answers. But recruiters face a persistent challenge: **how do you know what's real?**

Studies show that up to 78% of candidates embellish their CVs, from inflated GPAs to exaggerated project roles. For companies, this means wasted interview hours, bad hires, and costly turnover.

## The Verification Gap

Universities invest enormous resources in assessing student performance — rigorous exams, supervised projects, peer-reviewed theses. Yet this rich, verified data rarely reaches employers in a useful format.

Instead, we rely on:
- **Self-reported grades** that can't be independently confirmed
- **Generic recommendation letters** that say little about specific skills
- **Portfolio links** with no way to verify authorship or contribution level
